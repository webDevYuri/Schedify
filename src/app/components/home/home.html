Do I need a card to get started?

You can explore the demo and set up your booking page for free. Payments are only required when you activate subscriptions, securely handled via Stripe.

Can I customize my availability?

Absolutely. Define working hours, appointment durations, buffers, and blackout dates to match your schedule.

Is client data secure?

Yes. Your data is protected using a modern Angular + Laravel stack, and all payments are secured through Stripe.
