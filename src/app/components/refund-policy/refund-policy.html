Our Refund Policy

We stand by the quality of Schedify. Our policy is designed to be fair, transparent, and easy to understand.

1. 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may request a full refund within the first 14 days of your initial subscription payment. This guarantee applies to your first-time purchase only.

2. General Refund Policy

After the initial 14-day guarantee period, all subscription payments are non-refundable. Refunds will only be considered in the case of duplicate charges or a technical error that prevents you from accessing the service. We do not offer refunds for partial subscription periods.

3. Free Trials & Cancellations

The 7-day free trial allows you to test our service with no financial commitment. You may cancel your trial at any time to avoid being charged. If you cancel a paid subscription, your access will continue until the end of the current billing cycle, and no further charges will be applied.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us with your account details and the reason for your request. You can reach our billing support team at billing@schedify.com.
