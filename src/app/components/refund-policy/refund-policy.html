Refund Policy

At Schedify, we strive to deliver the best scheduling and automation experience. This Refund Policy outlines how we handle refunds for subscriptions and services.

1. Subscription Fees

All subscription payments are billed in advance on a recurring basis, depending on the plan you select. Payments are non-refundable except as outlined below.

2. Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds may be issued only in cases of duplicate charges, technical errors preventing access to the service, or where required by applicable law. We do not provide refunds for partial subscription periods or unused features.

3. Free Trials

If your subscription includes a free trial, you may cancel before the trial ends to avoid being charged. Once the trial converts into a paid subscription, normal refund rules apply.

4. Cancellations

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Your access will continue until the end of the current billing cycle, after which the subscription will not renew. No refunds are provided for early cancellation.

5. Contact Us

If you believe you are eligible for a refund or have questions about this policy, please contact us at billing@schedify.com.
