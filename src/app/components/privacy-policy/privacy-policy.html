Privacy Policy

At Schedify, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information directly from you when you create an account or use our services. This includes:

  • Account Data: Your name, email address, password, and contact details.
  • Client Data: Information you enter about your clients (names, contact info, booking history).
  • Payment Data: Billing information such as credit card details (processed securely by a third-party provider).
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform, including pages visited, features used, and device information.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used to provide and improve Schedify's services. This includes:

  • To provide and maintain the service and its core features.
  • To process payments and manage your subscription.
  • To communicate with you about your account and important updates.
  • To analyze usage and improve the platform's functionality and performance.
  • To enforce our Terms & Conditions and protect the security of our service.

3. Data Protection and Security

We use a combination of technical, administrative, and physical security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. While we take every precaution, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

4. We Do Not Sell Your Information

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes. Your trust is our most important asset.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal and contractual obligations.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time by following the unsubscribe link in our emails.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The revised policy will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Modified" date. We will notify you of any significant changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@schedify.com.
